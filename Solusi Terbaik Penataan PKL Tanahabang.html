Solusi Terbaik Penataan PKL Tanahabang

Producer: Wulan Octaviani
Reporter: Utari Septavanty
Videographer: Savira Tiya Azzahra, Shifa Fauziah
Editor and Scriptwriter: Wulan Octaviani

The arrangement of the Tanah Abang street vendor by the Government of DKI Jakarta, Governor Anies Baswedan and Vice Governor Sandiaga Uno is an effort to arrange street vendors in Tanah Abang by closing the road and some sidewalks on Jalan Jatibaru, Tanah Abang from vehicle access.

The Ombudsman Commission criticized the management of Tanah Abang, which is still prone to maladministration. Closing the road to sell is considered to violate Pasal 12 Undang-Undang Nomor 38 Tahun 2004 concerning roads. Then, the Ombudsman urges, normalization must be carried out no later than 60 days after the recommendation is issued.
